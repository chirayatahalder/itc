Dental Clinic

Dental Proceedures Performed:

  1. Dentures: done to compensate for lost or missing tooth.
  2. Dental Filling: Materials used
    • Amalgam/Non-Poisnous Mercury (Appearance is silver coloured). Strong used on biting surfaces.
    • Composite fillings (Appearance is tooth coloured). Strong but weaker than Amalgam. Material used include powdered glass quartz, silica or other ceramic particles added to a resin base.
    • Porcelain inlays (Natural tooth coloured). Advanced CADCAM technology is used to design and prepare perfectly fitted porcelain inlays in just one or two visits.
    • Glass ionomer (tooth coloured). Used on baby teeth or non biting surfaces.
    • Gold inlays and onlays (gold coloured).
